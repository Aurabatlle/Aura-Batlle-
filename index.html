TERMS & CONDITIONS

Participation Terms

  • Players must be at least 18 years old or have parental consent to participate in cash tournaments.
  • All participants must register with valid information and maintain one account only.
  • Any form of cheating, hacking, or unfair advantage will result in permanent ban.
  • Tournament prizes are subject to taxation as per government regulations.
  • Aura Battle reserves the right to modify tournament rules and prizes without prior notice.

Payment Terms

  • All tournament entry fees are non-refundable once the tournament has started.
  • Prize money will be transferred within 48 hours of tournament completion.
  • Players are responsible for providing correct payment details for prize distribution.
  • Aura Battle is not responsible for any bank charges or transfer fees.
  • Minimum withdrawal amount is ₹100 for all payment methods.

Account Security

  • Players are responsible for maintaining the security of their account credentials.
  • Sharing accounts is strictly prohibited and may result in permanent ban.
  • Aura Battle will never ask for your password via email or chat.
  • Report any suspicious activity on your account immediately to our support team.

GAME RULES

General Rules

  • All matches must be played on Free Fire MAX version
  • Players must join the match within 5 minutes of tournament start time
  • Intentional disconnections may result in disqualification
  • Players must use their registered in-game name
  • Any form of teaming in solo matches is prohibited

Prohibited Actions

  • No use of hacks, cheats, or modified game clients
  • No account sharing or boosting
  • No use of VPN to manipulate matchmaking
  • No offensive or inappropriate in-game names
  • No match fixing or collusion between players

Prize Distribution

  • Prizes will be awarded based on final tournament standings
  • Tie-breakers will be determined by kill count then damage dealt
  • Players must claim prizes within 7 days of tournament completion
  • Unclaimed prizes will be forfeited after 30 days
  • All prize decisions by Aura Battle admins are final

Dispute Resolution

  • All disputes must be reported within 24 hours of tournament completion
  • Evidence (screenshots/videos) must be provided for all claims
  • Aura Battle will investigate and resolve disputes within 3 business days
  • False reports may result in account suspension
